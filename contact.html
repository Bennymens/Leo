Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available at checkout. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 business days depending on location.
What is your return policy? +
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery, for items in original condition with tags attached. Simply use our returns portal online or drop your parcel at any of our partner locations. Exchanges are always free.
How do I find my perfect size? +
Each product page includes a detailed size guide with waist, hip, and inseam measurements. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit or sizing down for a sleeker, slimmer silhouette.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk ordering? +
Yes! We work with boutiques, retailers, and stylists. For wholesale inquiries, please select "Wholesale" in the contact form above or email us directly at wholesale@leojeans.com. Minimum order quantities apply.
Are your jeans sustainably made? +
Absolutely. We use certified organic cotton blends, water-recycling wash techniques, and partner only with ethically certified manufacturing facilities. Sustainability is at the core of everything we create — not just a label.
