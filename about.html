Who We Are

We Live &
Breathe Denim

At LEO, denim isn't just fabric — it's a philosophy. Every stitch is intentional, every wash is perfected, every fit is designed to move with you through whatever life throws your way.

Founded with a simple belief: that great jeans should be accessible to everyone. We source only the finest raw denim from certified mills, work with skilled artisans, and use sustainable practices that respect both people and the planet.

From our first pair of classic straight-cuts to our latest experimental washes, every collection tells a story of craft, culture, and community.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Craftsmanship

Every pair is cut, sewn, and finished by skilled artisans who take pride in their work — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Sustainability

We use water-saving wash techniques, organic cotton blends, and recyclable packaging to protect the planet we all share.

Inclusivity

Denim is for everyone. Our sizes, cuts, and styles are designed to celebrate every body type and personal expression.

Innovation

From stretch-engineered fabric to signature fades, we constantly push what denim can do — and what it should feel like.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

2013
The First Stitch

LEO was founded in a small studio with one sewing machine, a clear vision, and an obsession with the perfect pair of jeans.

2015
First Collection Launch

Our debut collection of 5 core denim styles sold out in 48 hours — validating what we always believed: people crave quality.

2018
Going Green

We transitioned to certified sustainable cotton and water-efficient wash processes, cutting our water usage by over 60%.

2021
Global Expansion

LEO Jeans began shipping internationally to 25+ countries, bringing premium denim to customers across Europe, Asia, and beyond.

Today
Still Growing

With 50,000+ customers and 120+ styles, we're still driven by the same passion that sparked that very first stitch.
